Offered Courses

Jain University offers 58 undergraduate and 51 postgraduate courses under 25 departments. Some of the courses offered by the university include BCA, BCom, B.Des, B.Tech, BA, MA, BBA, MBA, BMS, B.Sc, and Ph.D. Jain University also provides UG and PG distance education programs and online bachelor's, master's, and certification programs.

Campus Settings

Jain University is based in Bangalore nearby Bangalore Central. The university has 7 thriving campuses with over 18,310 aspiring students from more than 43 countries mentored by 993+ faculty members Jain Deemed to be University offers both on-campus (JGI Global Campus) and off-campus accommodations in different locations in Bangalore for students.
